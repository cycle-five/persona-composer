Endpoint of your running persona-composer (standalone server or the SillyTavern plugin).

⚠️ Off by default. When on, the panel shows a button that submits the post on your behalf after a confirmation. Auto-posting is squarely against X/Instagram terms and carries real account risk.

This extension reads the page DOM (against the letter of X/IG terms). With auto-post off, nothing is ever posted for you — you copy or insert, then Post yourself.
