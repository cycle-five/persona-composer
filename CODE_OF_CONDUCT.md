# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant][homepage], version 2.1, as its
code of conduct. We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free
experience for everyone, and we expect all participants — contributors,
maintainers, and anyone else taking part in the project's spaces — to uphold it.

Please read the full text here:
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

## Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the maintainers at **hello@cyclefive.xyz**. All complaints will be
reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and reporters' privacy and
security will be respected.

[homepage]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org
